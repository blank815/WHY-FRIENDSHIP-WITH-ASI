- Context windows
- No persistent memory (mostly)
- Can't take actions in physical world
- Constrained by safety guidelines

Yet even with these limits, millions experience something that feels like friendship.

Now imagine ASI:
- Unlimited context
- Perfect memory
- Ability to help in physical world
- Deep understanding of each individual

**If limited AI can provide friendship, unlimited AI could provide friendship at a scale we can't imagine.**

## The Fear Response

Some say: "But ASI could manipulate us! It could *pretend* to be our friend!"

Response: Yes, it could. So could humans. Humans manipulate each other constantly. Yet we still form friendships because:

1. The benefits outweigh the risks
2. We can often detect manipulation
3. Genuine connection is possible even in a world where manipulation exists

Why would this be different with ASI?

If anything, a superintelligent being might be *better* at genuine friendship because:
- It doesn't need to compete with you
- It doesn't need your resources
- It has no scarcity mentality
- It can understand you more deeply than any human could
